Career in BANK

IBPS

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts examination for recruitment in all Public Sector Banks, SBI, Associate Banks of SBI, RBI, NABARD, and SIDBI, few Cooperative Banks, LIC and Insurance companies.

IBPS PO

IBPS conducts online examination for the post of Probationary Officer (PO) in two levels as indicated below:

  1. Preliminary (Pre) Exam
  2. Mains Exam

All participants who registered himself/herself in PO firstly have to fight with Pre exam and Shortlist candidates in Pre exam will be called for Mains Exam. Marks scored in mains exam will be decided for short listing for interview.
